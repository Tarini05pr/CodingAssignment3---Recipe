Ingredients

  • Choice of yogurt
  • Granola
  • Mango
  • Peaches
  • Blueberries
  • Honey

Equipment

  • Mason Jar
  • Spoon
  • 1/3 Measuring Cup
  • Knife

Instructions

  1. Chop all fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Layer 1/3 cup yogurt at the bottom of jar.
  3. Add granola and fruit.
  4. Drizzle honey.
  5. Repeat layers until full.
  6. Garnish and enjoy!
